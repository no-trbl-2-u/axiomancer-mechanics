# Combat mechanics

## Advantage system

During combat the player and the enemy must decide between these "Heart, Body, or Mind" at the start of the turn. The player's advantage is then determined.

`Heart > Body > Mind > Heart`

### Advantage vs. Neutral vs. Disadvantage 

Each one has varying effects on different aspects of combat.
