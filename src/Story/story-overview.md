# Story Overview

## Places

### The World (NL)
The World has several continents, some of which are still unknown, and are mostly ruled internally. However, there is one city who has more power than any of them. It's the largest city in the world and has a labyrinth surrounding it (NBS). At the center of the labrynthian city is a castle where the King resides. The King is said to have been ruling over the lands for over 500 years. The many entrances to the Labyrinth have been closed and locked for over 100 years.

 
## Characters

### Main character/Little Boy (NL)
The main character is a little (13 years old) boy who lives in a small fishing village with only his father. He spends his days mostly fishing, lounging, and spending a lot of his time with his best friend (also nameless *) since childhood. They adventure along the coast. They have their head "in the clouds" and want to be remembered for being brave adventurers. The boy's father wants him to be a quiet, peaceful fisherman.

## Plot

### Central Plot
It is rumoured that the King's advisor has died and the King is looking for a new one, which is why the many entrances to the Labyrinth have opened for the first time in over 100 years. The King's advisor is, among many, considered the most prestigious and powerful position in the world. The main driving factor of the game is that the little boy wants to become the next advisor to the King.

### Order of Events
- Location: Fishing Village
    - Introduction: Boy, Girl, Father, Fishing Village
    - Interaction: Girl must move to a new village down the river and across a lake
    - Interaction: Boy decides to travel to her. 
    - Event: Father provides basic equipment (book, axe, tent, cart of fish)
    - Main Quest: Boy must build boat
    - Objective: Travel to Northern Forest
- Location: Northern Forest
    - Gathers wood (introduces combat mechanics, introduces "Gather Events/Map Nodes")
    - Objective: Travel to caves in Northen forest
- Location: Caves in Northern Forest
    - Event: Boy witnesses ceremony of a boy his age being crowned by the people living in the cave
    - Objective: Gathers Ore
    - Objective: Travel to Northern City beyond the forest
- Location: Northern City
    - Interaction: Trade cart of fish, wood, and iron to Blacksmith for services
    - Interaction: Boy hears rumor of the death of the King's advisor
    - Event: Boy witnesses the same ceremony again
    - Interaction: Boy acquires boat
    - Objective: Boy travels down the river to an island village
- Location: Island Village
    - Event: Boy witnesses ceremony once more
    - Interaction: Boy discovers that the ceremony is the selection of a village's representitive/candidate to travel to the Central Labrynthian city to become the new advisor to the King.
